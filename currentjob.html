About My Current Job....

Hello, my name is Damian Tapp. I have been working for Monroe Australia for 27 years. I actually started at Walkers Exhausts in 1993 and slowly worked my way to the TubeMill (Which I'm currently working at), I have a forklift and overhead crane licence. I moved to Monroe in 2003, to work on the new TubeMill they bought because the one at Walkers was being removed. I asked my boss to locate me there as I didn't want to stay there anymore. So he got me out and I have been there ever since. As I have gotten older and more mature, I feel as though I'm ready to move on from a factory worker to something more rewarding..
